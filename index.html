For the first ten years of my life, I lived in Amsterdam, Holland. I emigrated with my family to the U.S. on the "Leerdam", a Dutch passenger/freight ship. Throughout my life, I enjoyed pencil sketching and did a little painting. Recently, in my eighties and looking for something to do in my retirement, my daughter Katrina came to the rescue with a small painting set and said to me, "Dad, you can do this."
I hope you enjoy the paintings.

If you're interested in purchasing a painting or want to know more, contact me at OpaErnest@gmail.com
